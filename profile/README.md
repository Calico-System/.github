# Calico

A personal home lab system. One intelligence, three ways of being present.

---

## The system

Calico is the name for the whole thing. It runs on TrueNAS with Docker, sits on a home network, and does what it does quietly. Not all of it is custom built — some parts are off-the-shelf tools that Calico works through. The custom parts are here.

## The personalities

Calico has three personalities. They are not separate systems — they are different ways the same intelligence shows up in the world.

| Personality | Role | Communicates by |
|-------------|------|-----------------|
| **Birch** | The Gardener | Text — watches, reports, listens |
| **Oak** | The Warden | Sound and voice — speaks and listens |
| **[Unnamed]** | The Architect | Action — things just happen |

Birch and Oak appear across multiple interfaces. The Architect works in the background.
